You may already know that Scottish Water are making a few improvements to the drainage in the area so we wanted to get in touch with some handy tips to make things a little easier for you to get to the Leisure Club.

Scottish Water need to access the drains on Ewanfield Road which is one of the entrances to the hotel so this will be closed to vehicles from 31st August until 14th December (although you can quite happily pass by on foot).

We’d recommend arriving via our Ferntower Road entrance and continuing round past  Reception before parking as usual in the West car park. If you have limited mobility, we have some signposted spaces available across from our main Reception. 

If there's anything else you need, just ask the team next time you are in or give us a call on 01764 651624.
